Bomberman

Bomberman is a game with bomberman, bombs, and enemies.

Acquire points by killing enemy balloons.

The end goal is to destroy blocks and find the exit gate.

How to play:

W: To move Bomberman up.

A: To move Bomberman left.

S: To move Bomberman down.

D: To move Bomberman right.

Space Bar: To plant a bomb under Bomberman.

Enter: To restart the game once out of lives!

Esc: To exit the game.
